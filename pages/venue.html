Where:

The wedding ceremony and reception will be held at the Dharma Shed in the Wairau Valley, a short way from Blenheim.

The wedding itself will be outside (weather permitting). Seating will be provided for those wise owls with creaky joints, and picnic blankets for the rest. With this picnic plan in motion, please be mindful of what you wear; Will you be comfortable sitting on the ground? - nothing is too casual, I myself will be donned in a simple woollen robe, leather sandals, and a crown of thorns.

Directions:

Getting to the Dharma Shed is fairly easy from Blenheim:

  1. Follow state highway 6 towards Renwick.
  2. Just before Renwick take a left turn onto state highway 63
  3. Follow this road for several km, keeping an eye out for "Church Lane" on your left.
  4. Turn left down Church Lane
  5. Over the bridge, down the hill and first gate on your left!
